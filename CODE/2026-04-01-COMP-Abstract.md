## **Webinar Abstract**

As AI-assisted development accelerates, organizations face a new class of regulatory and compliance challenges—especially when AI-generated code enters systems subject to formal audits. This webinar demystifies the emerging risks and provides a clear framework for navigating them. We'll examine how AI-generated code disrupts traditional expectations around traceability, validation, documentation, reproducibility, and change control, and why these gaps matter to auditors and regulators.

Participants will gain a cross-industry view of the standards most affected—including FDA software validation expectations, ISO/IEC 62304, ISO 14971, SOC 2, SOX, PCI-DSS, and GDPR—and learn how AI-generated code complicates compliance obligations. The session concludes with practical, auditable mitigation strategies: tool qualification, human-in-the-loop review, automated testing, lifecycle controls, and risk-based governance models that enable teams to adopt AI responsibly without compromising regulatory readiness.

This webinar equips engineering leaders, compliance teams, and auditors with the clarity and controls needed to safely integrate AI into modern software development.
